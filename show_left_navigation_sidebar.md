# How to Show Left Navigation Sidebar in New Postman Interface

If your left sidebar containing Collections, Environments, and History disappeared after the recent Postman update, the software has defaulted to its new minimalist sidebar-free view. Follow these quick steps to bring it back.

### 1. Locate the Bottom Right Menu
* Move your cursor to the **very bottom right corner** of the Postman application.
* Find the status bar row next to the text options for **Globals**, **Vault**, and **Tools**.

### 2. Click the Sidebar Toggle Icon
* Click the **first icon** in that layout group cluster.
* The icon visually resembles a **small rectangle with a vertical line on the left side**.
* Clicking this will instantly slide open your left navigation panel.

### 3. Use the Keyboard Shortcut (Alternative Method)
* Skip the menu clicking entirely by using Postman's built-in global toggle command.
* **Windows/Linux**: Press **`Ctrl + \`**
* **Mac**: Press **`Cmd + \`**
