# How to Create a Workspace in Postman (Step-by-Step)

## Step 1: Open Postman
Launch Postman and sign in to your account.

## Step 2: Open the Workspace Menu
Click your current workspace name in the top-left corner.

## Step 3: Create a Workspace
Click **Create Workspace**.

## Step 4: Enter a Name
Type a name for your new workspace.

*Adding a summary or description is optional.*

## Step 5: Choose the Workspace Type
Select the workspace visibility:
- **Personal** – Only you
- **Team** – Your team
- **Partner** – External collaborators
- **Public** – Anyone

Choose **Personal** for this tutorial.

## Step 6: Click Create
Click **Create**.

## Step 7: Verify the Workspace
Your new workspace opens automatically. You can now create collections, environments, and API requests.
